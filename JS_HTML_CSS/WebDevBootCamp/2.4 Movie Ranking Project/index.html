The Best Movies According to Joe

My top 3 movies of all time:

Braveheart

William Wallace sparks a Scottish revolution against the English
after an English Lord executes his wife. He leads a growing militia
of Scottish countrymen through numerous battles in a fight for
freedom from English tyranny.

The Dark Knight

A wealthy businessman, Bruce Wayne, doubles as a masked
vigilante at night to fight crime and corruption in Gotham City.
Bruce, along with his alter ego, Batman, struggle with moral
dilemmas and decisions of good and evil, right and wrong dangled
by the notorious Joker, a not so serious, clown costumed sociopath.

Skyfall

James Bond is aging and being doubted by the powers that be
at MI6. To save the world from tyranny, he must duel with
a past MI6 operative gone rogue. Armed with the same training as Bond
the villain maintains a step on Bond until Bond takes the operation
off-script and into his own hands to counter. Bond is taken to his past
where the lives of those questioning his resolve are now in his hands.
